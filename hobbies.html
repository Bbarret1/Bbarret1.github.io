I Snowboard

I learned to snowboard in early 2012. From the moment I got on the slopes I have loved every minute of it. I have been to Killington, Pico, Toggenburg, and Whitetail, but Killington is my favorite because of it's shear size. When I find the time, and the money I like to coast on the blues, or see how fast I can run the greens. Click on some of the images to see some of the shots from the mountain. See you on the slopes.
